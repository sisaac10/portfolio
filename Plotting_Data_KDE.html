All four datasets are in this single data frame and the 'dataset' indicator is one of the columns. This form is often called tidy data, which is easy to manipulate and plot. In tidy data, each row is an observation and columns are the properties of the observation. Seaborn makes use of the tidy form.

Hexbin¶

Another way of dealing with too many data points is to bin them, which can be done by using jointplot() and setting the kind parameter.

In [30]:
sns.jointplot(np.log(subset['Votes']), subset['Rating'], kind="hex")
Out[30]:
<seaborn.axisgrid.JointGrid at 0x17a39523780>
