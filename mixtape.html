Environment

One of my favorite videos ever. All the feelings. Kevin Richardson articulates so many important ideas in this video.

One concept that Kevin stresses is that, "Habitat, Habitat, Habitat" is the key. Habitat loss is hurting our wildlife everywhere.

We have to do what we can to stop destroying habitat and do our best to restore habitat where we can. I will try and

post as much information as I can about helping to restore habitat and supporting related causes like wildlife corridors.
